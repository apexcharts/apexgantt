ApexGantt — Extended Column Catalog

Built-in columns beyond the basics: Predecessors and Successors (derived from the dependency graph), Assignees, and baseline Start / End / Variance. Each is sortable (click a header) and filterable.

What to look at
  • Predecessors / Successors show related tasks by WBS, with a non-FS type and lag suffix (e.g. 2SS+1d).
  • Variance compares each task's finish to its baseline end (+ = late, - = early).
  • Click a column header to sort by it; the baseline enabled below draws the striped baseline bars too.
