What to look for

  • Pale gray bands behind Sat/Sun — the weekend stripes from workingWeekdays.
  • Pinker bands for the two configured holidays — hover for a tooltip.
  • Duration column shows 5 wd not 7 d — calendar-aware.
  • Drag a bar onto a weekend. Its working-day duration is preserved across the snap.
  • Resize a bar across a weekend. Only the moving endpoint snaps; the other side stays put.
  • Summary "Design phase" aggregates child progress by working-day weight, not raw calendar days.
  • Critical path respects the calendar: weekend gaps between FS-linked tasks don't inflate the path.
  • Toggle Calendar: on/off to compare. With calendar off, every day is a working day, no stripes, raw drag.
