ApexGantt — Grouping

Group the task grid by any field. While grouping is active the parent/child tree is suspended and tasks are bucketed under collapsible group headers, each showing a member count.

How to interact
  • Use the Group by buttons to group by Team, Priority, or Progress.
  • Click a group header chevron to collapse/expand that group.
  • Clear grouping restores the original task hierarchy.
  • Grouping composes with the search box in the chart toolbar (filtering drops non-matching members).
