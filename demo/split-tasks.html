ApexGantt — Split Tasks

A task can be split into worked segments separated by gaps on a single row. The overall span, dependencies, and drag/resize all keep working — the segments ride along.

How to interact
  • "Implementation" starts pre-split into two worked spans with a gap.
  • Split "Testing" cuts it at Jun 18 and resumes Jun 22 (a 3-day gap).
  • Drag a split bar — all its segments move together. Resize a handle — the outer segment adjusts.
  • Clear split restores a single contiguous bar.
